# Research Plan: Scenario 008 - Annual Review Upsell Opportunity Identification

## Scenario Summary
The annual review is the most critical "strategic touchpoint" in the corporate banking relationship lifecycle. Instead of a routine credit check, expert RMs use this event to conduct a deep-dive "Wallet & Strategy Audit." By synthesizing year-over-year performance, wallet share gaps, and client strategic shifts, the RM transitions from a service provider to a strategic advisor. The goal is to identify $1M+ upsell opportunities by spotting where the client's growth has outpaced the bank's share.

## Research Objectives
Primary: Map the expert RM's "Pre-Review Intelligence" process for identifying high-value upsell opportunities.
Secondary: Identify specific data synthesis frameworks, "Upsell Triggers," and pre-meeting brief templates used by top-tier global banks.

## 7 Key Research Questions
1. **The "Annual Audit" Framework:** How do expert RMs structure the annual review to move from "compliance" to "commercial growth"?
2. **Wallet Gap Analysis:** What specific formulas and benchmarks are used to calculate "unlocked potential" during a year-over-year review?
3. **Upsell Triggers:** What data signals (revenue growth, geographic expansion, M&A mentions) are the most reliable indicators of an upsell opportunity?
4. **The "Review Brief" Architecture:** What are the components of a "Top-Performing" pre-meeting brief that RMs use to prepare for the C-Suite?
5. **Client Psychology & Objection Handling:** How do experts position an upsell during an annual review without it feeling like a "sales push"?
6. **Internal Benchmarking:** how do RMs use peer-client data to justify product recommendations to a client (e.g., "Your peers use X to manage Y")?
7. **Success Metrics:** What are the KPIs for a "Successful Annual Review" (e.g., Target vs. Actual Revenue, New Product Pipeline, Relationship Score)?
