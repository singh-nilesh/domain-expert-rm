# Research Plan: Scenario 013 - Digital Banking Product Adoption Push

## Scenario Summary
In the era of "Open Banking" and "Embedded Finance," a relationship is only as strong as its digital integration. Expert RMs view digital adoption not as a "cost-saving" measure for the bank, but as a "Strategic Moat" that creates high switching costs and deep operational efficiency for the client. This scenario focuses on identifying clients who are "Under-Digitized"—relying on manual portal uploads, phone-based FX, or paper checks—and pitching an "Integrated Digital Roadmap." The goal is to move the client toward API-based treasury, automated reconciliations, and AI-driven liquidity forecasting, increasing the bank's "Primacy" and reducing churn.

## Research Objectives
Primary: Map the expert RM's framework for driving digital product adoption and integrated treasury solutions.
Secondary: Identify specific "Digital Maturity" signals, ROI models for ERP-to-Bank integration, and best practices for "Digital-First" client onboarding.

## 7 Key Research Questions
1. **The "Digital Maturity" Assessment:** How do expert RMs audit a client's current treasury workflows to identify manual friction points?
2. **The Link Between Digital & Churn:** What is the industry benchmark for "Relationship Longevity" in integrated vs. non-integrated clients?
3. **Integrated Treasury ROI:** How do RMs quantify the value of API integration (e.g., time saved, reduced error rates, faster reconciliation)?
4. **Overcoming "Digital Inertia":** What are the most common client objections to digital transformation (e.g., security, IT cost) and how do experts handle them?
5. **The "API-First" Pitch:** How does an RM transition a client from a legacy web portal to an automated host-to-host or API connection?
6. **Data Signals for Adoption:** What behavioral signals (e.g., frequent login errors, high volume of manual wires) indicate a client is a prime candidate for a digital push?
7. **Success Metrics:** What are the KPIs for a "Digitally Entrenched" relationship (e.g., % of transactions via API, Daily Active Digital Users)?
