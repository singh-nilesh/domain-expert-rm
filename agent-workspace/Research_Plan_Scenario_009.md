# Research Plan: Scenario 009 - Lost Business Recapture Campaign

## Scenario Summary
Winning back a lost client or recapturing a declining wallet share is 5x more difficult than retaining an existing one, but has a 10x higher ROI than acquiring a cold prospect. Expert RMs view "Lost Business" not as a failure, but as a "Strategic Re-entry Point." By analyzing the "Churn Anatomy" and addressing the root cause (Pricing, Service, or Competitor), the RM builds a tailored "Win-Back" campaign. The goal is to restore the relationship to "Primary" status using data-driven incentives and strategic commitment.

## Research Objectives
Primary: Document the expert RM's framework for identifying, analyzing, and recapturing lost or declining corporate business.
Secondary: Identify specific "Win-Back" incentive models, "Churn Triggers," and proactive "Retention Scripts" used by top-tier commercial banks.

## 7 Key Research Questions
1. **The "Churn Anatomy" Framework:** How do expert RMs distinguish between "Natural Attrition" and "Competitor Theft"?
2. **Root Cause Analysis (RCA):** What data signals in transaction patterns (Monthly Profitability Aggregate) are the most reliable indicators of imminent or past churn?
3. **The "Recapture Pitch":** What are the components of a high-conversion "Win-Back" proposal (Pricing, Product, or Value-Add)?
4. **Psychology of the Win-Back:** How do experts manage the "loss of face" or relationship friction that led to the churn?
5. **Churn Prediction (Look-alike Analysis):** How do RMs use a "Lost Business" event to identify other at-risk clients in their portfolio?
6. **Incentive Structures:** What are industry-standard "Welcome Back" offers or pricing tiers for recaptured business?
7. **Success Metrics:** What are the KPIs for a successful recapture campaign (Time to Recovery, Recaptured Revenue %, Relationship Longevity)?
