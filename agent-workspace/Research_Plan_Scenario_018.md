# Research Plan: Scenario 018 - Margin Improvement Through Product Mix Optimization

## Scenario Summary
Relationship Managers (RMs) often face the "Volume Trap"—where large-volume lending clients generate high top-line revenue but sub-par margins due to thin credit spreads and high capital consumption. Expert RMs view these relationships through a **"Value-Driven"** lens, systematically identifying opportunities to layer on high-margin ancillary products that improve the overall Return on Equity (ROE). This scenario focuses on auditing the product mix of "Credit-Heavy" clients and pitching a "Strategic Bundle"—integrating FX Hedging, Cash Management, and Advisory services—to move the relationship from a commodity utility to a high-alpha partnership. The goal is to achieve a **200bps+ improvement in relationship margin** by shifting the fee-to-interest ratio.

## Research Objectives
Primary: Map the expert RM's framework for optimizing product mix to drive margin expansion.
Secondary: Identify specific "High-Margin" product categories, RAROC optimization formulas, and "Solution Bundling" best practices used by top-tier corporate banks.

## 7 Key Research Questions
1. **The "Volume-to-Value" Framework:** How do expert RMs audit a "Credit-Heavy" relationship to identify low-margin leakage?
2. **Identifying High-Margin Ancillaries:** What are the most profitable products per unit of Risk-Weighted Asset (RWA) (e.g., FX vs. Escrow vs. Agency services)?
3. **The "Bundling" Value Prop:** How do experts pitch a product bundle (e.g., "The Treasury Plus Pack") to justify a higher overall price point to the client?
4. **RAROC Optimization:** What specific levers (Tenor, Collateral, Ancillary Fees) have the highest impact on improving a client's Risk-Adjusted Return on Capital?
5. **Psychology of Price Negotiation:** How do RMs handle the "Rate-Shopping" client who only values the lowest loan spread?
6. **Data Signals for Optimization:** What signals in the core schema (Monthly Profitability Aggregate, KPI History) indicate a "Margin Expansion Opportunity"?
7. **Success Metrics:** What are the KPIs for "Product Mix Optimization" (e.g., Fee-to-Interest Ratio, Ancillary Capture Rate, Marginal ROE)?
