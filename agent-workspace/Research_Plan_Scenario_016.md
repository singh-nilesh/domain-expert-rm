# Research Plan: Scenario 016 - Leveraging Client Migration for Product Expansion

## Scenario Summary
The "First 90 Days" after a client transitions from a prospect to an existing customer is the most critical window for relationship deepening. Expert Relationship Managers (RMs) do not view "Winning the Mandate" as the end of the sale, but as the beginning of a "Product Expansion Sprint." This scenario focuses on identifying quick-win product additions—such as Transaction Banking, FX, or Wealth Management—that were not part of the initial "Anchor" pitch but are high-value needs for the new client. By using a "First 90 Days" action plan and tracking early transaction velocity, the RM ensures the bank captures 100% of the "Stated Objectives" before competitors can launch a counter-offensive.

## Research Objectives
Primary: Map the expert RM's framework for deepening newly won relationships during the migration phase.
Secondary: Identify specific "Quick-Win" product triggers, "First 90 Days" onboarding templates, and best practices for transitioning from a "Sales Team" to a "Relationship Team."

## 7 Key Research Questions
1. **The "90-Day Sprint" Framework:** How do expert RMs structure the first 90 days of a new relationship to maximize cross-sell velocity?
2. **Identifying "Unstated" Needs:** What data signals during the initial onboarding (e.g., KYC files, account opening patterns) reveal products the client didn't ask for?
3. **The "Anchor-to-Ancillary" Path:** What are the most common "Product Pairings" for new clients (e.g., if we won on Lending, what is the #1 next product)?
4. **Transition Psychology:** How does an RM successfully manage the hand-off from the "Acquisition Sales" team to the "Service/Coverage" team without losing momentum?
5. **Onboarding as a Sales Tool:** How do expert RMs use the implementation phase (e.g., setting up H2H or FX portals) to "soft-pitch" additional services?
6. **Data Baseline Verification:** How do RMs use the "First 30 Days" of transaction data to validate or revise the initial "Wallet Estimate"?
7. **Success Metrics:** What are the KPIs for "Onboarding Excellence" (e.g., Time to Second Product, 90-Day Revenue vs. Pro-Forma, Client NPS)?
