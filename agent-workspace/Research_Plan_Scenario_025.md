# Research Plan: Scenario 025 - Collateral Valuation Review Trigger

## Scenario Summary
In the world of secured lending, the value of the collateral is the bank's ultimate "Safety Net." Expert Relationship Managers (RMs) recognize that static valuations are a major source of hidden risk, especially in volatile real estate and equipment markets. This scenario focuses on the systematic management of collateral revaluation—utilizing automated policy triggers (e.g., 12-18 month limits) and market-driven "Out-of-Cycle" reviews. By synthesizing "Exposure-at-Default" (EAD) data, current market indices (e.g., Case-Shiller, Machinery Auction prices), and client financial health, the RM ensures that **Loan-to-Value (LTV)** ratios remain within internal guardrails. The goal is to proactively identify "Collateral Shortfalls" and negotiate for additional security or accelerated pay-downs before a market correction turns a technical breach into a realized loss.

## Research Objectives
Primary: Map the expert RM's framework for managing the collateral valuation and review lifecycle.
Secondary: Identify specific "Revaluation Triggers," the impact of LTV drift on Risk-Weighted Assets (RWA), and best practices for managing "Appraisal Gaps" in a declining market.

## 7 Key Research Questions
1. **The "Valuation Policy" Benchmark:** What are the standard regulatory and internal policy thresholds for revaluing Real Estate vs. Equipment vs. Receivables?
2. **Identifying "Market Shocks":** How do expert RMs use external indices (e.g., MSCI, Bloomberg Commodity Index) to trigger an "Out-of-Cycle" valuation review?
3. **The LTV Drift Mechanic:** How does a 10% drop in collateral value affect the bank’s regulatory capital and RWA for a typical senior secured loan?
4. **Appraisal Management:** How do expert RMs choose between a "Desktop Valuation," a "Broker Price Opinion" (BPO), and a "Full MAI Appraisal"?
5. **Handling the "Shortfall":** What are the tactical steps for a "Margin Call" on a corporate borrower? How do experts negotiate for additional "Pledge of Assets"?
6. **Data Signals for Success:** What signals in the CRM (Negative Issues/Action summary, slowing revenue) indicate that a client's specific collateral (e.g., a specialized factory) is losing value?
7. **Success Metrics:** What are the KPIs for "Collateral Management" (e.g., Average LTV Drift, Time-to-Revalue, Collateral-to-Loss ratio)?
