# Research Plan: Scenario 017 - Dormant Account Reactivation

## Scenario Summary
Dormant accounts represent "Lazy Capital" on the bank's balance sheet—consuming KYC resources and relationship management time without generating offsetting revenue. Expert RMs view dormancy not as a "dead end," but as a diagnostic challenge. This scenario focuses on identifying the root cause of dormancy—whether it is service-driven, competitor-led, or a result of a client's strategic pivot—and deploying a tailored "Re-engagement Playbook." The goal is to either "Fix" the relationship by reactivating a core operating line or "Exit" gracefully to free up capital for higher-performing clients.

## Research Objectives
Primary: Map the expert RM's framework for identifying and reactivating dormant corporate accounts.
Secondary: Identify specific "Dormancy Triggers," reactivation incentive models (e.g., "Welcome Back" fee waivers), and "Exit" protocols for non-strategic accounts.

## 7 Key Research Questions
1. **The "Dormancy Anatomy" Framework:** How do expert RMs distinguish between "Seasonal Hibernation" and "Permanent Attrition"?
2. **Identifying Root Causes:** What specific fields in CRM call reports and transaction logs reveal why a client stopped using the bank (e.g., service dispute vs. pricing)?
3. **The "Reactivation Hook":** What are the most effective product hooks for re-engagement (e.g., automated cash sweeps, new FX platform)?
4. **Psychology of the Re-entry Call:** How do experts manage the "neglect" conversation if the bank hasn't called the client in 12 months?
5. **Incentive Structures:** What are industry-standard "Reactivation Offers" (e.g., 6 months of waived wire fees) used to incentivize a return?
6. **Decision Logic (Fix or Exit):** What are the criteria for a "Graceful Exit" (e.g., ROE below hurdle, non-strategic sector, high KYC cost)?
7. **Success Metrics:** What are the KPIs for a "Reactivation Campaign" (e.g., Reactivation Rate, Incremental Revenue per Reactivated Client, Portfolio ROE Improvement)?
