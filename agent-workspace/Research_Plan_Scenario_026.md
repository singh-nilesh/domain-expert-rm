# Research Plan: Scenario 026 - Covenant Breach Early Warning

## Scenario Summary
Financial covenants (e.g., Net Leverage, Interest Coverage, DSCR) are the "Tripwires" of a credit agreement. Expert Relationship Managers (RMs) do not wait for a covenant certificate to show a breach; they utilize **Pulse Monitoring** of monthly management accounts and internal KPIs to predict breaches 60–90 days in advance. This scenario focuses on the identification of "Covenant Cushion" decay—where a client's performance trend indicates an imminent breach. By synthesizing "Financial Ratio and KPI History" with macroeconomic sector shocks, the RM develops a "Pre-emptive Cure Plan." The goal is to initiate a proactive restructuring—such as an "Equity Cure," a covenant reset, or a temporary waiver—that protects the bank’s seniority and capital while providing the client with the operational "Breathing Room" needed to execute a turnaround.

## Research Objectives
Primary: Map the expert RM's framework for predicting and mitigating covenant breaches.
Secondary: Identify specific "Cushion Benchmarks," the mechanics of modern "Restructuring Levers" (Equity Cures, PIK Toggles), and best practices for the "Amendment & Extension" (A&E) process.

## 7 Key Research Questions
1. **The "Covenant Cushion" Metric:** How do expert RMs calculate the "Safety Margin" (e.g., % head-room) and at what level (e.g., <10%) does it trigger a "High-Alert" status?
2. **Identifying "Phantom" EBITDA:** How do experts audit "Add-backs" and "Pro-forma synergies" to find hidden covenant stress in "Covenant-Lite" agreements?
3. **The "Early Intervention" Script:** How does an RM successfully pivot a standard relationship call into a sensitive "Imminent Breach" discussion?
4. **Restructuring Levers Menu:** What are the pros/cons of a "Covenant Reset" vs. a "Permanent Waiver" vs. an "Amortization Holiday"?
5. **The "Equity Cure" Mechanic:** What are the standard terms for an equity injection to "fix" a ratio? How many times can a sponsor use this lever?
6. **Data Signals for Success:** What lead-lag signals (e.g., A/P stretching, slowing inventory turnover) are the most reliable predictors of a ratio breach?
7. **Success Metrics:** What are the KPIs for "Covenant Management" (e.g., Cushion Stability, Breach Resolution Time, Amendment Fee Yield)?
