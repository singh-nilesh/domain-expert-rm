# Research Plan: Scenario 021 - Product Bundle Discount Strategy

## Scenario Summary
In the commoditized lending market, "Price" is a race to the bottom. Expert Relationship Managers (RMs) break this cycle by utilizing **Product Bundle Discounts**—a strategic pricing model where the cost of the "Anchor" product (e.g., a loan) is contingent on the client’s adoption of high-margin ancillaries (e.g., Cash Management, FX). This scenario focuses on structuring a "Relationship Pricing Grid" that rewards "Wallet Consolidation." By offering a "Spread Discount" for every additional product line used, the RM incentivizes the client to move from a fragmented multi-bank setup to a "Primary" relationship. The goal is to maximize the bank’s **Relationship ROE** while providing the client with a lower **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO).**

## Research Objectives
Primary: Map the expert RM's framework for structuring and pitching product bundle discounts.
Secondary: Identify specific "Pricing Grid" templates, bundle components (e.g., The "Treasury Pack"), and the ROI impact of bundling on client retention and RWA efficiency.

## 7 Key Research Questions
1. **The "Relationship Pricing" Model:** How do expert RMs structure a tiered discount grid (e.g., "Bronze, Silver, Gold" tiers based on product count)?
2. **Identifying "Bundle Hooks":** Which product combinations have the highest correlation with relationship longevity (e.g., Lending + Integrated Receivables)?
3. **Calculating the "Step-Down" Margin:** How do RMs determine the optimal discount (in bps) for each ancillary added without dropping below the hurdle rate?
4. **The "Primacy" Value Prop:** How do experts pitch the "Total Cost" benefit of a bundle vs. the "Line-Item" cost of a competitor?
5. **Managing Revenue Cannibalization:** How do banks ensure that a discount on the loan spread is fully offset by the incremental fee income from the bundle?
6. **Data Signals for Bundling:** What signals in the core schema (Low Fee-to-Interest Ratio, High Transaction Volume) indicate a client is a prime candidate for a bundle pitch?
7. **Success Metrics:** What are the KPIs for a "Successful Bundle" (e.g., Ancillary Capture Rate, Relationship ROE Lift, Churn Reduction %)?
