# Research Plan: Scenario 015 - Incremental Revenue Targeting from Existing Products

## Scenario Summary
In many corporate banking relationships, the client is already using multiple products but at "sub-optimal" levels (e.g., only 30% of payment volume, 20% of FX flow). Expert Relationship Managers (RMs) view these as the most efficient growth opportunities because the "Cost of Acquisition" is zero. This scenario focuses on identifying the "Internal Wallet Gap"—where a client's business growth has outpaced the bank's share of their existing product usage. By benchmarking the client against "Peer Group Adoption" and quantifying the revenue uplift of moving from "Secondary" to "Primary" status, the RM can secure high-margin incremental revenue without the need for new credit approvals.

## Research Objectives
Primary: Map the expert RM's framework for identifying and capturing incremental revenue from existing products.
Secondary: Identify specific "Product Utilization" benchmarks, "Wallet Gap" formulas, and the best practices for pitching "Relationship Primacy" to an existing client.

## 7 Key Research Questions
1. **The "Internal Audit" Framework:** How do expert RMs audit current product utilization to identify "Low-Capture" lines?
2. **Wallet Gap Formulas:** What specific formulas are used to estimate the "Total Possible Wallet" for Cash Management, FX, and Trade Finance?
3. **Peer Benchmarking:** How do RMs use "Peer Adoption" data (e.g., "90% of your peers use our automated receivables tool") to drive incremental sales?
4. **The Psychology of "The Second Product":** How do experts handle the objection of "Don't put all your eggs in one basket" (multi-banking preference)?
5. **Data Signals for Expansion:** What behavioral signals (e.g., spike in transaction volume, geographic expansion) indicate a client is ready to move more flow to the bank?
6. **Quantifying the Value of Primacy:** How do RMs calculate the "Consolidation Benefit" (e.g., lower fees, better data visibility) for a client who consolidates their flow?
7. **Success Metrics:** What are the KPIs for "Incremental Growth" (e.g., Product Penetration Index, SOW Growth %, Incremental ROE)?
