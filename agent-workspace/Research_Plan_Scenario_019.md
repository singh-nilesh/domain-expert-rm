# Research Plan: Scenario 019 - Event-Driven Sales Triggers

## Scenario Summary
In the high-velocity corporate banking market, the difference between a "Lead Bank" and a "Participant" often comes down to **Speed-to-Lead.** Expert Relationship Managers (RMs) operate as "Signal Intelligence" hubs, using automated monitoring of external data—news feeds, SEC filings, M&A announcements, and regulatory updates—to identify banking opportunities before the client even realizes they need a partner. This scenario focuses on the taxonomy of "Event-Driven Triggers" (e.g., a new factory opening in a foreign region triggering a Trade/FX need) and the strategic "Outreach Window" (the 24-48 hours after an event occurs). The goal is to position the bank as a proactive strategic partner that delivers solutions in real-time, effectively "front-running" the competition and securing high-value mandates.

## Research Objectives
Primary: Map the expert RM's framework for identifying and converting event-driven sales triggers.
Secondary: Identify specific "Trigger Categories," the ROI of proactive outreach, and the "Precision Banking" tools used by top-tier institutions to automate signal detection.

## 7 Key Research Questions
1. **The "Signal Taxonomy":** What are the most reliable external triggers for corporate banking (e.g., M&A rumors, ESG mandates, Geographic expansion)?
2. **Speed-to-Lead Benchmarks:** How does the probability of winning a mandate decrease as time passes after a public announcement?
3. **The "Trigger-to-Product" Mapping:** Which events lead to which specific banking products (e.g., "New CFO" $\rightarrow$ Treasury Audit; "Capex Announcement" $\rightarrow$ Project Finance)?
4. **Automating Detection:** What technologies (NLP, Sentiment Analysis, Web-scraping) do expert banks use to surface these triggers for RMs?
5. **The "Outreach Script":** How does an RM successfully position a "cold" insight-led call based on a news event without sounding like a "paparazzo"?
6. **Data Synthesis:** How do RMs cross-reference external signals with internal wallet data to prioritize their outreach?
7. **Success Metrics:** What are the KPIs for "Event-Driven Sales" (e.g., Trigger Conversion Rate, Time-to-Mandate, Relationship Velocity)?
